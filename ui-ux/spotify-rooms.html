Spotify Rooms

June 28 - July 16, 2019

To solve the reocurring problem my friends and I have of playing music in the car, I decided to make a prototype feature I would want introduced into the Spotify mobile application called Rooms. Rooms would create a new, fast way of sharing music for any occassion, whether that be in the car or at a party.
Through this project, I learned Sketch, InvisionApp, and how to make gifs in Photoshop. This was also my first time integrating svgs on to a webpage.
To learn more, visit: www.matthewkwong.com/spotify-rooms
