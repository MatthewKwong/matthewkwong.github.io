Help the Homies

Novemeber 2 - 4, 2018

Developed at Cal Hacks 5.0, the largest collegiate hackathaon, 2 friends and I created Help the Homies. Help the Homies is a web application designed to help homeless people in your local community. I came up with the idea after noticing the on-going homeless crisis in San Francisco.
I worked on the back-end JavaScript, learning how to implement the Google Maps API and Firebase.
To check it out, visit: www.matthewkwong.com/homeless-helper
