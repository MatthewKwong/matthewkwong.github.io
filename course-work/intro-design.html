Design 1 - Intro to Design

September 25 - December 7, 2018

In my first design course, I learned about major designers and their signfiicance to how we view good design today. Additionally, I also learned what it means to design ethically, the importance of making things affordable and accessible for everyone, and what the concept of design obsolescence.
As a final project in groups of 4 we were to make a new product based on 1 of 8 categories from Emily Pilloton's book, Design Revolution. My group was assigned the Play category and built a new, affordable, fun game called Flick It.
